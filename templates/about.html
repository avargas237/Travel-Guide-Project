Who We Are

TBD is a service where you can view all the travel information you need to travel the world without unpleasant surprises.

By searching for any location in the world, be it an address, a tourist attraction, or simply a city, we provide you with updated and relevant information to plan your itinerary.

TBD currently offers the following insights for any travel destination:

  • Nearby webcams: now you can see if the location is really how it seems, or if it is largely inactive due to the pandemic.
  • COVID-19 risk determination: check the risk level, COVID statistics, and travel advisories for your next getaway.
  • Hotel offers nearby: Depending on your budget, party size, and dates, you can view the nearest best lodging options tailored for you.
  • Airfare: Depending on when and where your departure is, you can view the best flight deals to get you from point A to point, well, TBD!
